To handle backpropagation, we take the derivative of the activation function with respect to the gradient, forcing us not to derive the vector against itself, but on the output of the gradient, i.e.

dO/dGradient = output*derivative(input) by the chain rule.

Parameters
in activations of layers
out error terms (recursion gradients)
Returns
the hessian

Reimplemented in Softmax.
